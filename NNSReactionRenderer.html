Text Reaction Renderer (Plain Text Species)

Format example: r2_2, 100, 1, COONa, 1, Cu2+, k1, 1, COOCu+, 1, Na+
Species are rendered as normal text (not LaTeX). Lines starting with # are comments: rendered as text, not parsed as equations, and not equation-numbered.
Load input TXT by clicking Load TXT (handle also the case of pointing to other TXT files), select all related TXT files at once, then choose the main TXT and click Load selected main file. The main file is the file that starts to refer other files but no files refer it. It is the parent of all children files.
